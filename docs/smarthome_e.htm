Smart Home Research

Integration of HEMS (Home Energy Management System) and IoT Technology

What is Smart Home

Smart Home is a next-generation residential system that combines IoT technology with energy management systems. Our laboratory aims to realize efficient energy utilization centered on HEMS (Home Energy Management System) and create comfortable living environments.

Energy Management

Integrated management of distributed energy resources such as solar power generation, batteries, and electric vehicles to achieve efficient electricity utilization.

IoT Integration

Connect home appliances, sensors, and control devices through networks for real-time monitoring and control.

Smart Control

Automatic control utilizing AI and machine learning to achieve optimization tailored to residents' lifestyle patterns.

Main Research Areas

Renewable Energy

Optimization of small hydroelectric power systems and efficient energy management through coordination with battery storage

Wearable Device Integration

Lifestyle behavior measurement using wearable devices and lifestyle improvement advisory systems

Environmental Monitoring & Control

Achieving comfortable living environments and improving lifestyle behaviors through automatic adjustment of temperature, humidity, and illumination
