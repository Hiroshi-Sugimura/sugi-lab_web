Welcome

Let me introduce myself briefly. I am engaged in research and development of smart home systems utilizing IoT technology.

Research Overview

Smart Home & HEMS

I am engaged in research and development related to residential environments. Specifically, I conduct research on software development, measurement, control, and visualization for Smart Home and HEMS (Home Energy Management Systems).

IoT Technology Development

I conduct research on technology development for connecting everyday objects (Things) to computer networks, represented by IoT (Internet of Things).

Data Mining

I also develop mining technologies that discover knowledge from time series data. I work on building prediction systems utilizing machine learning.

Research Policy

Let's start by getting our hands dirty and creating something.

I emphasize a practical research approach, advancing learning and research not only through theory but also through the construction of actually working systems.
