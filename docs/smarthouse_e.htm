Overview

For developing HEMS (Home Energy Management System), smart house and smart life, we study and develop a system for supporting inhabitants by using network-connected home appliances, wearable device, and any IoT devices.

For example, An ECHONET Lite compatible device is developed by Arduino. The network environment is following.
